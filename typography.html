Typography Info

Tillana is a Sans-Serif typeface designed by the Indian Type Foundry. It is a unique font that has a hand written feel to it, with a lot of personality and character while supporting both Devanagari and Latin scripts.
It has a wide variety of weights ranging from regular to extra bold, making it a versatile typeface that can be used for a variety of design projects. A couple unique details about it is that all the letters are on a 10 degree slant giving it a more dynamic feel and all the vertical strokes have a slight swell in their tops and their bottoms.

This site was designed and programmed by Warrington Connor. It showcases the Tilliana typeface throught three different compositions.
The first composition uses the typeface to design an image. It uses 5 different characters oriented in different positions to design an abstract image. The second composition showcases what I would think that the natural habitat of the typeface would be. This typeface gave me a more mysterious vibe so I decided to design something that gave a similar mysterious feel. The third composition is just a showcase of the character list and my favourite character in the typeface.
